About 10/10



The 10/10 Alumni Summit 2022 is the first-of-its-kind event in India to celebrate the visionary leadership culture among IIT alumnus. IITs are known to produce prominent world leaders such as Sundar Pichai (Google CEO), Parag Agrawal (Twitter CEO), Arvind Kejriwal (Delhi CM, founder AAP) and Raghuram Rajan (ex RBI Governor).

The award is conferred every two years to recognize and honor the 10 most influential alumni of IITH for their visionary outlook.

The award intends to recognize their accomplishments along with their visionary thinking. A jury of distinguished leaders from different geographies and diverse domains will examine the nominations to recognize the finest visionaries.
To celebrate this, we will have a Visionary Leadership Summit with an official magazine and press release.

Our Vision



The vision of the 10/10 Alumni Award and Leadership Summit is to support and promote world class visionary leadership that impacts India and the world. It also aims to make visionary leadership thinking a part of culture and society.
